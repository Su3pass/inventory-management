Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new item?

Go to the "Items" page, fill out the form with the item details, and click "Add Item".

How can I edit an existing item?

On the "Items" page, find the item in the list and click the "Edit" button. Make your changes and click "Update".

How do I search for items?

Go to the "Search" page and type in the search box. The results will update as you type.

Troubleshooting

Items not saving

Make sure you've filled out all required fields (marked with *) before submitting.

Search not working

Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, check your internet connection.

App looks strange

Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
