Help Center Frequently asked questions How to add a new item?

1. Navigate to the 'Add' page

2. Fill in all required fields

3. Click the 'Add' button to submit

 How to search for items?

1. Navigate to the "Search" page

2. Enter the item name in the search bar

3. The results will be automatically filtered and displayed

 How to update or delete items?

1. Navigate to the 'Update' page

2. Search for the item to be edited

3. After modifying the information, click "Update" or click "Delete" to remove the item
